Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026. Questions? support@zealplus.app

This Cookie Policy explains how Zeal+ uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website (zealplus.app). This policy does not cover the Zeal+ mobile app, which does not use browser cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They help the site remember information about your visit — like your preferences — and can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

We also use similar technologies like local storage and session storage for the same purposes.

Cookies We Use

Required

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function. These enable core functionality like page navigation and cannot be disabled.

Optional

Analytics Cookies

Anonymous page view data that helps us understand how visitors use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected.

We do not use advertising cookies. We do not place cookies for ad targeting, retargeting, or any third-party advertising network.

Third-Party Cookies

The following third-party services may set cookies when you visit our website:

  • Google Fonts — loads the Outfit typeface used across the site. Google may set performance cookies. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

We do not embed third-party social media widgets, tracking pixels, or ad scripts on this website.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Block all cookies (note: this may break some website functionality).
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser.

Browser-specific instructions

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect the functionality of this website.
