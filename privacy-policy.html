Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026. Questions? support@zealplus.app

Zeal+ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Zeal+ app or website, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile data — fitness level, training style, available equipment, and body metrics you enter during onboarding or in settings.
  • Workout logs — exercises, sets, reps, weights, durations, and notes you record.
  • Support communications — emails or messages you send to our support team.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information — operating system, device model, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data — features accessed, workout generation events, session length, and in-app navigation patterns.
  • Crash reports — diagnostic data to help us identify and fix bugs.
  • IP address and general location — used for fraud prevention and analytics.

Health Data (with Your Permission)

If you grant permission, Zeal+ syncs with Apple Health (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) to read and write workout, activity, and body measurement data. This data is used only to power app features and is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized workouts tailored to your goals, fitness level, and equipment.
  • Track your progress and display workout history and trends.
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Send important service notifications (password resets, policy updates).
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your health data, workout logs, or personal information with third-party advertisers.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate the app (cloud hosting, payment processing via RevenueCat, analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer to the successor entity under the same privacy protections.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure — if you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately at support@zealplus.app.

6. Children's Privacy

Zeal+ is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & portability — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate data in-app or by contacting us.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of analytics — disable analytics in app Settings.
  • Health data access — revoke Apple Health or Health Connect permissions at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@zealplus.app.

8. Third-Party Services

The app and website use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management and purchase tracking.
  • Google Fonts — font delivery (website only).
  • Apple Health / Google Health Connect — health data integration (optional, device-level).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we'll notify you via in-app notification or email. Continued use of Zeal+ after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
