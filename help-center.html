Getting Started

Zeal+ is available on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). Search "Zeal+" or tap the download buttons on our home page. The app is free to download — create an account and start training immediately.

After downloading, the onboarding flow walks you through setting your training style, fitness level, available equipment, and goals. You can update any of these settings later by tapping your profile icon and selecting Settings.

Zeal+ supports 9 training styles: Strength, Hypertrophy, CrossFit, Hyrox, Hybrid, HIIT, Bodyweight, Endurance, and Mobility. Free users get access to 3 styles. Zeal Pro unlocks all 9.

Workouts & Plans

Tap the orange + button on the home screen. Adjust your preferences in the settings drawer — training style, muscle focus, duration, and available equipment — then tap Generate. Your personalized workout is ready in seconds.

Yes. During a workout, long-press any exercise to open the Swap Sheet. Zeal+ suggests biomechanically equivalent alternatives that target the same muscles. You can filter swaps by equipment you have available.

The Plan Builder (Zeal Pro) lets you create multi-week training programs tailored to your goals. Set your weekly schedule, training split, and target duration, and Zeal+ generates a full progressive plan. Access it from the Plans tab.

Tap the calendar icon in the bottom navigation to access your history. Free users can view the last 30 days. Zeal Pro unlocks unlimited history and detailed progress charts.

Billing & Zeal Pro

Zeal Pro is $5.99/month or $39.99/year (save 44%). Both plans include all 9 training styles, the full Plan Builder, unlimited workout history, and all future Pro features.

On iOS: open the App Store → tap your profile → Subscriptions → Zeal+ → Cancel. On Android: open the Play Store → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Zeal+ → Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google based on their policies. On iOS, request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com. On Android, visit the Play Store and select the subscription for refund options. Contact us at support@zealplus.app if you need help.

Health Sync

Zeal+ prompts you to connect Apple Health during onboarding. If you skipped it, go to your iPhone's Health app → Sharing → Apps → Zeal+ and enable the data types you want to share. Workouts sync automatically after that.

On Android, open Health Connect (or search for it in the Play Store if not installed), go to App permissions, find Zeal+, and enable the desired data types. You can also manage this from Zeal+ Settings → Health Sync.
