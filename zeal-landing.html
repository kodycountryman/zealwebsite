Gentle, joint-friendly training. Higher reps, lower loads, and controlled movements for sustainable fitness.

Help Center

Getting Started

Download Zeal+ from the App Store or Google Play. Complete the onboarding to set your training style, fitness level, and available equipment — then tap the + button to build your first workout.

Generating a Workout

Tap the orange + button on the home screen. Adjust your settings in the Settings drawer — style, split, duration, and equipment — then hit Generate. Your workout is ready in seconds.

Zeal Pro

Tap your profile icon and select "Zeal Pro" to upgrade. Pro unlocks all 9 training styles, the full plan builder, unlimited history, and more for $5.99/mo. Cancel anytime from your App Store or Google Play subscription settings.

Apple Health / Health Connect

Zeal+ syncs automatically with Apple Health on iOS and Health Connect on Android. Grant permission when prompted during onboarding, or enable it anytime in your device Health app settings.
